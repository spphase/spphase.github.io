The 12th Guangxi University Student Computer Application Contest "Su Xin Ge" theme website technical description document
This website is based on the theme of "not forgetting the original heart, cultural self-confidence", and choosing culture to start the website content, aiming to introduce the unique culture of China. And represent the development of culture in today. Through the introduction of the six cultures, college students are encouraged to understand the traditional excellent culture and feel the charm of excellent culture, thus establishing recognition and confidence in Chinese culture, and then becoming a helper of cultural heritage. "Su Xin Ge" is divided into seven sections, of which the first six sections introduce poetry, Hanfu, cultural relics, music, architecture, and film. The last section introduces today's cultural policies and situations.
